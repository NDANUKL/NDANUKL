### ✨ about me

i'm a fourth-year BSc Informatics & Computer Science student at **Strathmore University**, Nairobi, building at the intersection of cloud, web, and AI. i believe technology should be a tool for people, especially the ones who don't have it yet!

- 🔭 &nbsp;**Currently building:** an offline-first academic assessment system for Kenyan secondary schools, powered by AWS + ML

- ☁️ &nbsp;**AWS Student Buider Group Leader** at Strathmore's AWS Student Builder Group

- 🔗 &nbsp;**Former Integration Lead** @ Web3Clubs Kenya where I built an AI-powered prediction market trading bot

- 🌱 &nbsp;**Exploring:** sync architectures, Random Forest models, and everything in between

- 💬 &nbsp;**Ask me about:** cloud infra, full stack development, or community-building
